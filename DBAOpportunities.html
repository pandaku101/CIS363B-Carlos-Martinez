Why did I go into the field

Reasons I want to be a DBA

Job Security

As long as organizations rely on databases to store and manage their data, database administrators will be needed. This contributes to job security in the field.

Competitive Salary

Database administrators often receive competitive salaries due to the specialized nature of their role. Experience and expertise in managing databases, especially for large enterprises, can lead to well-compensated positions.

Continuous Learning

The field of database administration is dynamic, with new technologies and methodologies regularly emerging. This provides DBAs with continuous learning and professional development opportunities, keeping their skills relevant.
